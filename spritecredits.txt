I have tried to ensure only sprites with use permission were included, if you would like your art removed from this project please let me know
There is some ambiguity of who should be credited for certain overworld sprites. While I have tried to attribute credit correctly, please let me know if you have not been credited correctly
Unless stated, no sprites have been edited (aside from minor edits to maintain 16 colour pallete indexing and 80*80/32*32 sizing). 
Where directions of overworld sprites have been missing I have made some basic placeholders (Glacia, Drake, Brandon, Tucker, Greta, Spenser) which are missing feet animations, as is Noland
All new trainer sprites are missing animations
